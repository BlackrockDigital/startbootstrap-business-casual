about me

My name is Irina and I am an ever-ongoing artist. I say ever-going because I have a family now and I have been a stay-at-home mother but continue to work on things I love. That ranges from painting, creating leggings with bleach/drawing, sewing clothes for my son and many other random hobbies. I am and will always be an artist at the center of it all.
